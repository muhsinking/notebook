kak·i·sto·crat (/kaˈkistəˌkrat/)

**Noun:** A very ill-qualified or corrupt ruler; a member of a **kakistocracy**, which is a government run by the least qualified, most corrupt, or worst individuals. Comes from the Greek _kakistos_ (meaning "worst") and _-cracy_ (meaning "rule" or "government").
