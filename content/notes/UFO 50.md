I can't believe this game exists.

## Highlights

- **Warptank**
	- AKA VVVVVV 2.
	- Puzzles are genius.
	- This game must be like 10 hours long wth.
- **Mooncat**
	- Incredible idea; wonderful execution.
	- Some of my favorite [music](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mPpBECMrBpc&list=PLgFVH9V-HKYmL3PCUGzhc1SCUCXll9SzM&index=28) in the whole collection.
- **Party House**
	- Essentially a push-your luck type board game.
	- Absolutely adorable sprites & sounds.
- **Avianos**
	- 4X game about The Bird Wars, where you take actions by praising various dinosaur deities.
	- Reminds me of Master of Magic (1994), perhaps my favorite 4X of all time.
	- Perfect. No notes.
	- OK one note: I want more.
- **Devilition**: 
	- AKA Into the Breach 2.
	- Boiled my brain.
	- Kind of hate the way it looks.
- **Paint Chase:**
	- Pac-man meets Splatoon.
- **Waldorf's Journey:**
	- What if jumping was hard and bad, but that was fun.
	- Bennet Foddy-esque.
- **Vainger**:
	- Metroidvania is such a GOATed genre.
	- But the final boss sucks IMO.

## Lowlights

- Quitting out of **Mooncat** 30 minutes in, and then realizing the game has no save function.
- **Porgy**: Adore the look, but not sure where to find the fun.
- **Golfaria**: I'm just bad at it, okay.
