Something fun I saw [on twitter](https://x.com/i_zzzzzz/status/1408546824970031126?lang=en). The goal is to try to experience at least three of the so-called nine delights ever day:

- **Walking around**: Self explanatory
- **Fellowship**: Hanging out with friends
- **Deliciousness**: Having a lil treat
- **Transcendence**: Feeling like you've "leveled up"
- **Goofing**: Having a laugh
- **Amelioration**: Working towards the betterment of something, e.g. developing a skill
- **Coitus**: Self explanatory
- **Enthrallment**: Becoming incredibly engaged in something; hyperfocusing
- **Wildcard**: Anything delightful not mentioned above
